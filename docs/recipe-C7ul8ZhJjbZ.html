Zutaten

Spice mix

  • 3 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 2 tablespoons cumin
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cayenne

Chicken/Marinade

  • 48oz chicken breast
  • 240g yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons ginger paste
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1/2 of spice mix

Masala sauce

  • 30g butter
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons ginger paste
  • 4 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 of spice mix
  • 14.5oz can tomatoes
  • 150ml water
  • Handful chopped cilantro/stems
  • 200g low fat Greek yogurt
  • 15g butter
  • Handful chopped cilantro
  • to taste Salt
  • 200g basmati rice
  • 350g Chicken bone broth
  • 11 burrito tortillas

Herby Yogurt sauce (for dipping/served w burritos)

  • 600g low fat greek yogurt
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 80ml milk
  • 15g honey
  • Chopped Chives/cilantro

Zubereitung

  1. Use half in marinade, and half in sauce
  2. Cook onions over high heat until translucent/beginning to brown, add garlic/ginger and saut√© for 3-4 minutes
  3. Simmer for 1 min
  4. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, blend until completely smooth
  5. Add to blended sauce and mix over low simmer
  6. Grill chicken until charred, about 10 minutes (or bake/broil). It should be slightly undercooked.
  7. Dice, add to sauce, cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes
  8. I cooked the rice in bone broth for extra protein + flavor, optional
  9. Place meat/rice mixture in a bowl and refrigerate for ~20 minutes. This helps it thicken/makes rolling burritos 100x easier
  10. Reheat instructions: remove from freezer, wrap in a paper towel, microwave for 2 mins, then air fry at 375 for 6-8 minutes to crisp up your tortilla, or pan fry

Notizen & Tipps

  • Every recipe is high protein, low calorie and insanely delicious - the Stealth Health way

  1. Use half of the spice mix in marinade, and half in sauce.
  2. For the Masala sauce, cook onions over high heat until translucent/beginning to brown.
  3. Add garlic/ginger to onions and saut√© for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add tomato paste and 1/2 of spice mix. Simmer for 1 min.
  5. Add can tomatoes, water, and handful of chopped cilantro/stems.
  6. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, then blend until completely smooth.
  7. Add 200g low fat Greek yogurt, 15g butter, handful chopped cilantro, and salt to taste to the blended sauce and mix over low simmer.
  8. Grill chicken until charred, about 10 minutes (or bake/broil). It should be slightly undercooked.
  9. Dice the chicken.
  10. Add diced chicken to sauce, cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  11. Cook 200g basmati rice in 350g Chicken bone broth (optional for extra protein + flavor).

Notizen & Tipps

  • Reheat instructions: remove from freezer, wrap in a paper towel, microwave for 2 mins, then air fry at 375 for 6-8 minutes to crisp up your tortilla, or pan fry
