Pancake Bowl

ü§ñ Zeit: 2.18s üìÖ Version: 2025-09-11_11-35-47

Zutaten

Per bowl

  • 1 egg
  • 50 g yogurt unflavoured (or vanilla)
  • 70 ml milk I use almond but any milk will work
  • 35 g flour all purpose
  • 25 g protein powder vanilla
  • drizzle (~1 tsp) honey
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

Zubereitung

  1. Top with fruits of your choice!!
  2. Bake for 20-25mins at 180c/350f
  3. You can top your baked pancake bowl with more honey/maple syrup if you wish to (optional)

Notizen & Tipps

  • there is very little sugar in this recipe for calorie reasons but you can add as much sweetner as you want, depending on how sweet you like things
  • These were my macros but it will depend on which protein powder you use, which yogurt you use, milk, amount of sweetner etc..
