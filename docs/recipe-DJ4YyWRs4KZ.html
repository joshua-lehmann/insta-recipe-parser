High Protein Cheeseburger Hotpockets

üçΩÔ∏è 8 Hotpockets ü§ñ Zeit: 3.86s üìÖ Version: 2025-09-11_11-35-36

Zutaten

Cheeseburger Beef

  • 400g lean ground beef (5% fat or less)
  • 1 medium diced white onion
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne (optional for added spice)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 100ml beef stock
  • 80g light cream cheese
  • 45g reduced sugar ketchup
  • 60g diced pickles
  • Cooking spray

Hot Pocket Dough

  • 400g plain flour
  • 410g 0% Greek yoghurt
  • optional salt to taste

Hotpockets assembly

  • 1/8 cheeseburger beef
  • 25g low fat grated cheese

Zubereitung

  1. Place in a pan with cooking spray on medium heat, cover with a lid and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side
  2. Wrap in foil and store in the freezer for months
  3. To reheat, unwrap from the foil, wrap in a damp paper towel and microwave for 3-4 minutes, unwrap carefully from the paper towel and air-fry or toast in the pan for a couple minutes to get crispy, enjoy!

Makes 8 Hotpockets:
Calories & Macros
Per Roll: 348 calories
31g P | 43g C | 5g F
Cheeseburger Beef
- 400g lean ground beef (5% fat or less)
- 1 medium diced white onion
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp cayenne (optional for added spice)
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 100ml beef stock
- 80g light cream cheese
- 45g reduced sugar ketchup
- 60g diced pickles
- Cooking spray
Hot Pocket Dough
- 400g plain flour
- 410g 0% Greek yoghurt
- optional salt to taste
Hotpockets assembly
- 1/8 cheeseburger beef (weigh out total mixture and divide by 8 for gram amount)
- 25g low fat grated cheese
Place in a pan with cooking spray on medium heat, cover with a lid and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side
Storage & Heating
- Wrap in foil and store in the freezer for months
- To reheat, unwrap from the foil, wrap in a damp paper towel and microwave for 3-4 minutes, unwrap carefully from the paper towel and air-fry or toast in the pan for a couple minutes to get crispy, enjoy!
